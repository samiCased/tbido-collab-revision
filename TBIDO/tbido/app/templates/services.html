What are the Services we Offer?

TBIDO aims to support and nurture early-stage technology-based startups by providing a comprehensive ecosystem that fosters innovation and entrepreneurship.

Startup Development Section

We spark Entrepreneurial journeys through Awareness, Design Thinking, Branding Support, and early Team-Market Readiness Level.

Technology Development Section

We assist R&D, Prototyping, Product Testing, with Expert Guidance, fueling Tech Refinement, and access to External Lab.

Incubation Management Section

We offer Tailored Business Mentorship, Registration, SRL Assessment, Seed Funding, and External Grants.

Enterprise & Development Section

We boost Exposure, Partnerships, Co-Working Space Access, and Network Opportunities for Startups.
